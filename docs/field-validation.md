# MapGap Stage 1C Field Validation

Use this guide for observed sessions. Do not explain the interface unless the
participant is unable to continue; record where assistance became necessary.

## Participants

Run at least 12 sessions across these viewpoints:

- relocation household;
- economic development or workforce practitioner;
- real-estate practitioner;
- corporate relocation or HR practitioner.

Record role and device class only. Do not collect names, exact home addresses,
employer-confidential information, or free-text prompts containing personal data.

## Journeys

### Jersey City Laundry

Ask the participant to find laundromats around Journal Square and decide whether
the area appears workable without a car. Do not mention the heatmap unless they
ask what practical access means.

### Capital Region Daily Life

Ask the participant to inspect libraries plus one other daily-life category in
Albany, Schenectady, or a nearby town, then share the resulting view.

### Participant-Selected Need

Ask the participant to move the map to a familiar place and use Custom category
for a need that matters to them.

## Session Measures

- Device and viewport class.
- Journey attempted.
- Seconds to first useful result.
- Completed without assistance: yes/no.
- Top-result relevance: relevant results / first ten inspected.
- Heatmap started, completed, abandoned, or not attempted.
- Source badge understood: yes/no/uncertain.
- Shared link reproduced the intended area: yes/no/not attempted.
- Next decision the participant wanted to make.
- Severity and concise description of any failure.

## Exit Review

Stage 1C passes when at least 80% of participants complete a useful search
without assistance in under 30 seconds, no severity-one mobile or routing issue
remains, and the evidence identifies a first paid workflow and purchaser.

Create the decision record from `docs/templates/pilot-decision-record.md` rather
than expanding categories without evidence.
